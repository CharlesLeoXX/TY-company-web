ABOUT US

Focusing on chair production for 20 years, it is a perfect combination of quality and design

TAIYU - Professional Manufacturer of dining chairs and lounge chairs

Since its establishment in 2005, we have been committed to the research and development and production of high-quality chairs. Our products combine ergonomic principles with modern design aesthetics to provide users with the most comfortable sitting posture experience.

We have advanced production equipment and a technical team. We strictly control every production process to ensure that every chair meets international quality standards. Whether it's lounge chairs, dining chairs or bar stools, we meticulously craft them all with a spirit of craftsmanship.
